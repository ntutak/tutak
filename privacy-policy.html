Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our SMS messaging service.

Information We Collect

When you opt in to receive text messages, we collect your mobile phone number. We may also collect message content and delivery metadata (e.g., timestamps, delivery status) as part of normal SMS service operation.

How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number and related information solely to:

  • Send you SMS messages you have requested or consented to receive
  • Respond to your replies or opt-out requests
  • Maintain records required for legal or regulatory compliance

SMS Messaging Consent

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive text messages from us. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or message data with third parties for marketing purposes. We use Twilio as our SMS delivery provider; Twilio processes message data on our behalf and is subject to its own privacy policy. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data Retention

We retain phone numbers and message logs only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Contact
